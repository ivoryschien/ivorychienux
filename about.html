Hi there! I'm a visual and product designer, and I graduated from the University of California - Santa Cruz with a degree in Studio Arts. I was born and raised in Taipei, Taiwan, and I immigrated to the United States when I was 19 years old.

Ever since I was young, I've had a deep curiosity about the world around me. I love exploring and experimenting with art at home, and I started learning how to draw when I was just 5 years old. Creating art, from blank paper to colorful masterpieces, has always been a source of joy for me. My natural talent and interest in art and design eventually led me to pursue a career in the field.

I discovered my passion for UI/UX design when I landed a position as a WeWave UI/UX designer in 2021. This experience inspired me to start my own business and explore product design and business. If you're interested, you can check out my full journey here.

In my free time, I enjoy taking my dog for runs, going on hikes, or just chilling at home and watching TV.
