Introduction

IvoryDigitalHub is an e-commerce platform that provides customers with fast and aesthetically pleasing digital stationery products. It offers a wide range of digital products, including planner templates, digital notebooks, and digital stickers that are preface for personal and professional use.

I built IvoryDigitalHub all by myself. IvoryDigitalHub was born during Covid-19, February 2021. Back in the time, I was suffering in finding a job. So I decided to start my own business at the same time I join WeWave as a product designer.

Goal

The digital planner is the future

The goal is to create a user-friendly and efficient notebook that enables users to easily organize, store, and access their notes, without the need for multiple notebooks or risking damage or loss of information. The objective is also to provide a sustainable solution that minimizes paper waste and promotes eco-friendly practices.

Requirements

User Testing

After testing the first version of my planner on the GoodNotes app, I received valuable feedback that allowed me to enhance my designs. One user expressed her desire for more templates to choose from, while also appreciating the streamlined navigation that allowed for faster access to different pages. However, she did not like the excessive amount of buttons with text on the planner. In response, I revisited my designs and incorporated more templates while also hiding certain buttons. To improve organization, I divided the templates into different sections and created an index page that allows for easy copying and pasting within the notebook.

Reflection

What I learned from this

Through the process of building a startup, I've come to learn invaluable lessons. To be honest, I never imagined myself as a business owner, and the challenges I faced were immense. I dedicated long hours to work, sacrificing leisure activities like watching TV or playing games. Yet, the rewards were worth it. Building everything by myself was far from easy, but the experience taught me not only about product design but also the intricacies of running a successful business.

Here comes more

IvoryDigitalHub continues to experience growth and expansion, with new products being developed and introduced every month. Looking ahead, the next milestone is to attain 100,000 followers on my social media accounts. This represents an exciting step forward in our ongoing efforts to connect with and engage a wider audience. I remain committed to providing innovative products and delivering value to our customers, and this is just one of the many ways we're striving to achieve that goal.
